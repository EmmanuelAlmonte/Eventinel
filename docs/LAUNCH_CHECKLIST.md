## 🚀 Pre-Launch Checklist

  - [ ] geteventinel.com (marketing redirect)

- [ ] Confirm app store approvals
- [ ] Stage all social posts
- [ ] Prepare email blast
- [ ] Brief team on launch plan
- [ ] Test all links

### Launch Day
- [ ] 🚀 Flip the switch (make live)
